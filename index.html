Sixteen structured skills that turn Claude, ChatGPT, or any capable LLM into a disciplined garage partner — diagnostics, maintenance, used-car buying, EVs, motorcycles, and more. Each skill defines what to ask, what context to give, and exactly where to stop trusting the machine.

Operating procedure

3 steps
STEP 1

Pick a skill

Warning light → OBD-II. Strange noise → Troubleshooting. Known repair → Mechanic Assistant. The index below routes you.

STEP 2

Give the vehicle context

Year, make, model, engine, mileage, conditions. Each skill tells you exactly what to gather — that context is what separates generic answers from useful ones.

STEP 3

Iterate, then verify

Run the cheapest discriminating test, report back, narrow down. Confirm the diagnosis before buying parts, and check specs against the factory manual.
